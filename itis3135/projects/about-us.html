About Me

Hello, my name is Min Park

I have proudly served as an Health Insurance Agent in Raleigh, North Carolina for over 30 years.

When I first came to the US, I was working different jobs ranging from venting chickens and a restaurant owner.

After I became a US Citizen, I expanded my career into health insurance, property and casualty insurance, real estate and mortgaging services.

Over the years, I worked under many renown companies like State Farm, GEICO, Blue Cross and Blue Shieldand Nationwide

Eventually, I specialized in health insurance and real estate where I continue to help clients to this day.


Aside from my career, I serve as a deacon at First Korean Baptist Church where I also work as a Korean-English translator and equipment tester.

I also provide assistance in helping Korean immigrants navigate through insurance policies and matters while also have several times helped translate testimonies in court.

In my free time I like to play golf, watch korean sitcoms, spend time with my family, and watch movies.
