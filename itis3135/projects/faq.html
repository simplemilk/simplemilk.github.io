What's your main demographic for your job?

For my job, I primarily work with Koreans due to my proficiency in the language and my connections with the church.

However, that's not the only community I work with! I work with latinos, chinese, and other Americans that wish to find good insurance plans!

What is the most common misconception you faced while working in health insurance?

The biggest misconception that I have encountered was regarding that of pre-existing conditions.

Pre-existing conditions is when you have health problems prior to having a health insurance.

Many people believe that from having pre-existing conditions causes their insurance rates to go up.

However with the passing of the affordable care act (ACA), people can contest their charges online as it's legal to allow this to occur.
