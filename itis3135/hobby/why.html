Why should you listen to KRnB?

KRnB is versatile, although it seems like one genre there is a lot of different kinds of artists in this umbrella.

As mentioned prior, there are people like DEAN that sing mainstream RnB music, DPR Live that plays EDM music, and Hoody that sings chill RnB.

Though that may be the case, there are actually even more artists that do not fit under these general labels such as BIBI that sings ballads and Jay Park that sings pop.
