What is this site about?

This site is a general hobby page looking into my personal interests in Korean Rhythmn and Blues (K-RnB).

When listening to artists in this genre, there are a couple of factors to consider when looking for an artist:

  1. Rhythm
  2. Instruments
  3. Mood
  4. Melody
  5. Lyrics

Based on these factors you can end up figuring out what kind of artists would fit that if your vibe such as the ones below:


Recommendations

Here are some artist recommendations I would listen to:

Dean (Vocal powerhouse)

One of the most influential K-RnB artists, with amazing vocal techniques and amazing runs, he was the one that definitely got me interested in the genre hands down.

With classics such as Instagram that showcased his vocal skills and lyrical composition.

As well his smooth break up song with the usage of drums and piano in What2Do (ft.Crush & Jeff Bernet).

Hoody (Like a Warm Sunny Day)

A chill laid-back artist that well-known in the music space that incorporates unique melodic sounds to create a relaxing memorable experience such as the usage of the HANGANG(Han River).

While incorporationg bouncy melodic beats with warm tones like D-Day(ft. GIRIBOY).

DPR LIVE (Experimental and Unique)

One of the most influential artists in the electronic music scene with the incorporation of psychadellic sounds and melodic rap with songs such as Geronimo! and Jasmine.
