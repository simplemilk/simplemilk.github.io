The difficulty of booking a concert

Generally for other genres of music, specifically KPop, it is pretty easy to find concert dates due to its immense popularity.

The other issue is that most of the concerts are generally in Korea so there is likely less artists willing to go out to have a concert in the US.

When it comes to mainstream artists, it should be easier to find them using TicketMaster, KPlay, and SeatGeeks.
